## 3. Copywriting Strategy & Market Differentiation
The copywriting approach for this niche shifts from pure technology specs to community utility marketing:
* **Data Privacy Reassurance:** Cyber cafe users often fear credential theft. Explicitly mentioning that *systems automatically clear history* addresses data privacy concerns upfront, building immediate trust.
* **Friction Elimination for Non-Tech Users:** Framing the business as a "Digital Partner" instead of an "Internet Parlor" changes the value proposition from a hardware rental space to an active, helpful solution hub.
* **Targeted Local Context:** Highlighting real local use-cases (exam forms, bill payments, university admissions) directly addresses the immediate practical needs of neighborhood residents.
